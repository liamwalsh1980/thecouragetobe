Proctor’s 3 Function Interactive Model (1986/1992)

FORMATIVE FUNCTION
(Learning)

Clinical supervision concerned with developing the skills, abilities and understandings of the supervisee

NORMATIVE
(Accountability)

Clinical supervision concerned with maintaining and ensuring the safety and effectiveness of the supervisee’s practice work

RESTORATIVE FUNCTION
(Support)

Clinical supervision concerned with how the supervisee responds emotionally to the stresses of the work
